Alappuzha Beach

One of the most popular picnic spots in Alappuzha, this beach is famous for an ancient pier and a lighthouse. The pier is over 140 years old and breathes history. Built by Captain Crawford, this structure gives you a rare experience of walking right into the sea!

Kuttanad

Kuttanad is historically important in the ancient history of South India and is the major rice producer in the state. Farmers of Kuttanad are famous for Biosaline Farming.
